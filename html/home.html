About Us

VacciNation is a socially relevant web application developed by 3 IT students from Anna University. We developed VacciNation in order to help parents or guardians all over the country to keep track of vaccination schedules of their kids. Although India is a leading exporter of vaccines, our country is home to one-third of the worldâ€™s un-immunized children. The usual practice for immunization is the immunization card provided by hospitals. This is only useful when the parents or guardians are already educated about the need for vaccination. Our solution takes it a bit further by alerting the hospital when a baby/child needs to be vaccinated by keeping track of all the vaccination records of the individual and the hospital staff can alert the parents/guardians via SMS or email.This method is not only helpful in pushing the parents and guardians to get their kid immunized, but it also helps educate them about the need of on-time immunization. Our main goal is to reach and alert people who have less to no access to smartphones and wireless broadband connection and those who are unaware about the importance of vaccnination.
